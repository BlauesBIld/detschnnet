Magedle


Holla citizen of the village franzhausen, the timeth hast cometh, thou art the only magician in thy village, and thy folk needeth thy holp m're than ev'r. An unpredictable uprise of monst'rs did occur and those gents tryeth to destroyeth thy village and killeth ev'ryone inside of it!

Defeat those folk and proveth yourself w'rthy to beest hath called the most wondrous magician of franzhausen!
